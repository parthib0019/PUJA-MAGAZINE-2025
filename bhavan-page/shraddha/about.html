~: About Us :~

Rooted in the values of Ramakrishna Mission Vidyamandira, Nabarun is a vibrant expression of creativity and community from Shraddha Bhavan, celebrating the spirit of Durga Puja through student contributions.

রামকৃষ্ণ মিশন বিদ্যামন্দির

Ramakrishna Mission Vidyamandira, located in Belur, is a esteemed educational institution under the Ramakrishna Mission, dedicated to holistic education combining academic excellence with spiritual and cultural values.

নবারুণ পত্রিকা

Nabarun is the annual Durga Puja wall magazine of Shraddha Bhavan, showcasing the creative contributions of the students through articles, poems, and songs celebrating Bengali culture and spirituality.

শ্রদ্ধা ভবন

Shraddha Bhavan is one of the hostels at Ramakrishna Mission Vidyamandira, fostering a vibrant community where students engage in cultural, academic, and especially spiritual activities.
